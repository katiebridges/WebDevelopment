ABOUT ME


I'm an analytics consultant at InterWorks, Inc., currently living in New Orleans, Louisiana. I have a background in Public Policy, and I love seeing people connect to their community through data. If you want to hang out, I'd love to grab a good cup of coffee, and get outside hiking, camping, biking, or sailing. In accordance with my southern roots, I love sweet tea, being barefoot, bonfires, and long summer nights. I'm a big fan of road trips and all the NPR that accompanies them.
